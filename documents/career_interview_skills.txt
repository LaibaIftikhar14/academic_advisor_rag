Job Interview Preparation Guide

Types of interviews:
- Phone screening: 15-30 minutes, basic qualification check
- Technical interview: Coding challenges, problem solving
- Behavioral interview: Past experience and soft skills
- Case interview: Business problem solving (consulting)
- Panel interview: Multiple interviewers simultaneously

Technical interview preparation:
- Practice data structures and algorithms daily
- Use LeetCode, HackerRank, and Codeforces
- Study system design for senior positions
- Practice explaining your thought process out loud
- Review your past projects thoroughly

Behavioral interview (STAR method):
- Situation: Set the context
- Task: Describe your responsibility
- Action: Explain what you did specifically
- Result: Share the measurable outcome

Common behavioral questions:
- Tell me about a time you faced a challenge
- Describe a situation where you worked in a team
- How do you handle conflicting priorities?
- Tell me about your greatest achievement
- Describe a time you made a mistake and how you handled it

Day of the interview:
- Arrive or log in 10-15 minutes early
- Dress professionally (research company dress code)
- Bring copies of your resume
- Have questions prepared for the interviewer
- Send a thank you email within 24 hours

Salary negotiation:
- Research market salary ranges before negotiating
- Never give a number first if possible
- Consider the full package: salary, benefits, growth
- It is always appropriate to negotiate - companies expect it
