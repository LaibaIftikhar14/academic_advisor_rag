Exam Accommodations for Students with Disabilities

Students with documented disabilities may be eligible for exam accommodations. These may include:
- Extended time (typically 1.5x or 2x the standard time)
- Separate quiet exam room
- Use of a computer for written exams
- Reader or scribe assistance
- Large print exam papers
- Frequent breaks

To receive accommodations:
1. Register with the Disability Services Office at the beginning of the semester
2. Provide documentation from a qualified healthcare professional
3. Request accommodation letters at least 2 weeks before each exam
4. Submit accommodation letters to each instructor

Accommodations are confidential and will not affect how instructors treat students academically.
