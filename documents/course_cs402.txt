Course: CS402 - Machine Learning
Credits: 3
Description: Supervised and unsupervised learning, regression, classification, clustering, model evaluation, and deep learning fundamentals. Python with scikit-learn and TensorFlow.
Prerequisites: CS401, MATH301
Assessment: 40% projects, 20% midterm, 40% final
Tips: Focus on understanding intuition behind algorithms. Implement algorithms from scratch before using libraries.
