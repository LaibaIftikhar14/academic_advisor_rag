Professional Ethics and Workplace Conduct

Professional ethics in technology:
- Data privacy: Handle user data with care and respect
- Security: Never compromise security for convenience
- Intellectual property: Respect copyright and licenses
- Conflict of interest: Disclose and avoid conflicts
- Honesty: Be truthful about capabilities, timelines, and problems

Code of professional conduct:
- Deliver work on time and communicate delays early
- Write clean, documented, maintainable code
- Do not take credit for others work
- Respect confidential information
- Follow company policies and legal regulations

Work-life balance:
- Sustainable pace is more productive long-term
- Set boundaries around working hours
- Take all your vacation time
- Disconnect from work during personal time
- Recognize burnout early in yourself and others

Diversity and inclusion:
- Treat all colleagues with equal respect
- Call out discriminatory behavior professionally
- Support diversity initiatives in your workplace
- Be aware of unconscious bias in hiring and promotions
- Mentor individuals from underrepresented groups
