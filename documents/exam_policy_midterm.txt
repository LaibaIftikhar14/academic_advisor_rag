Midterm Examination Guidelines

Midterm examinations are typically held during the 7th or 8th week of the semester. Exact dates are announced by instructors at the beginning of the semester.

Preparation tips:
- Review all lecture notes and textbook chapters covered so far
- Complete all practice problems from assignments
- Attend instructor review sessions
- Form study groups to discuss difficult concepts
- Visit office hours at least one week before the midterm

What to bring:
- Student ID card
- Pens and pencils
- Calculator (if permitted by instructor)
- No notes unless explicitly allowed (open book exam)

Results are typically returned within 2 weeks of the exam date.
