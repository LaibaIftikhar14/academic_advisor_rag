Complete Exam Preparation Guide

Starting early (3-4 weeks before exam):
- Create a study schedule covering all topics
- Identify which topics carry the most marks
- Gather all materials: notes, textbooks, past papers
- Form study groups with motivated classmates

Two weeks before:
- Begin active review of all topics
- Complete past exam papers under timed conditions
- Visit office hours to clarify difficult concepts
- Create summary sheets for each topic

One week before:
- Focus on weak areas identified from practice tests
- Review summary sheets daily
- Get 8 hours of sleep every night
- Exercise regularly to maintain energy

Day before the exam:
- Light review only - no new material
- Prepare everything needed for the exam
- Eat a proper meal
- Sleep at least 8 hours
- Avoid cramming late at night

Day of the exam:
- Eat a nutritious breakfast
- Arrive at the exam venue early
- Read all questions before starting
- Manage time carefully during the exam
- Check answers if time permits
