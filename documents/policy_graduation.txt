Graduation Requirements and Application Policy

Degree requirements:
- Minimum 120 credit hours for bachelor's degree
- Minimum GPA of 2.0 overall and in major
- Completion of all required core curriculum courses
- Completion of all major requirements
- Residency requirement: Minimum 30 credits at this university

Graduation application:
- Apply for graduation at least one semester before expected completion
- Deadline: October 1st for December graduation, February 1st for May graduation
- Application submitted through student portal
- Degree audit completed by academic advisor
- Any deficiencies must be resolved before graduation

Graduation ceremony:
- Held in May for spring graduates
- December graduates may participate in May ceremony
- Cap and gown rental required (available through bookstore)
- Guests: Maximum 4 tickets per graduate (additional available by lottery)
- Ceremony location: University main auditorium

Diploma:
- Mailed to address on file within 8 weeks of graduation
- Pick up available at registrar office
- Replacement diploma: $50 fee
- Official transcripts showing degree conferred available immediately after degree posted

Honors designations:
- Cum Laude: GPA 3.5 - 3.69
- Magna Cum Laude: GPA 3.7 - 3.89
- Summa Cum Laude: GPA 3.9 - 4.0
