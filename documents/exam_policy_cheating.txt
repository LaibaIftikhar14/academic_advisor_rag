Academic Integrity and Cheating Policy

The university takes academic dishonesty extremely seriously. The following actions constitute academic misconduct:
- Copying from another student's exam paper
- Using unauthorized notes or materials during an exam
- Communicating with other students during an exam
- Submitting work that is not your own
- Plagiarizing from online sources or AI tools without proper citation

Consequences of academic misconduct include:
- Zero grade for the assessment
- Suspension from the university
- Permanent expulsion for repeated offenses
- Notation on academic transcript

Students are encouraged to report suspected cheating to the academic integrity office.
