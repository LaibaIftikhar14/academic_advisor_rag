Financial Aid and Scholarships Policy

Types of financial assistance available:

Merit-based scholarships:
- Dean's List Scholarship: GPA 3.8 or above (full tuition)
- Academic Excellence Award: GPA 3.5-3.79 (50% tuition)
- Department scholarships: Contact your department for specific awards

Need-based aid:
- Submit FAFSA by February 1st for priority consideration
- Financial need determined by family income and assets
- Aid packages may include grants, loans, and work-study

Maintaining financial aid:
- Minimum GPA: 2.5 for most scholarships
- Full-time enrollment (12+ credits) required for most aid
- Satisfactory academic progress must be maintained
- Report any change in financial situation to financial aid office

Emergency financial assistance:
- Emergency loan fund available for unexpected financial crises
- Food pantry available on campus for food insecure students
- Emergency housing assistance for students facing homelessness
- Contact student affairs office for confidential assistance

Payment deadlines:
- Tuition due: 2 weeks before semester start
- Late payment fee: $150 after deadline
- Payment plans available through student accounts office
