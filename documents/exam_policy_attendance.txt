Class Attendance and Participation Policy

Regular attendance is mandatory at this university. The following attendance policies apply:

Minimum attendance requirement: 75% of all scheduled classes
- Falling below 75% attendance may result in automatic failure of the course
- Some courses require 80% or higher attendance

Legitimate reasons for absence:
- Medical illness (requires medical certificate)
- Family emergency (requires documentation)
- University-sponsored events (requires letter from relevant department)
- Religious observances (notify instructor in advance)

Recording absences:
- Students must inform their instructor before missing a class when possible
- Unexcused absences count toward the attendance threshold immediately
- Three late arrivals equal one absence in most courses
