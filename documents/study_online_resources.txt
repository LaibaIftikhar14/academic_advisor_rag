Online Learning Resources for University Students

Free academic resources available to students:

Video Learning:
- Khan Academy: Mathematics, science, and humanities
- Coursera: University courses (audit for free)
- MIT OpenCourseWare: Full MIT course materials
- YouTube channels: 3Blue1Brown (math), Crash Course, Professor Leonard

Programming Resources:
- freeCodeCamp: Web development and programming
- LeetCode: Coding interview practice
- HackerRank: Programming challenges
- GitHub Student Pack: Free developer tools

Research and Reading:
- Google Scholar: Academic papers
- JSTOR: Academic journals (check university access)
- Semantic Scholar: AI-powered research tool

Productivity Tools:
- Notion: Notes and project management
- Anki: Spaced repetition flashcards
- Forest App: Focus and productivity
- Grammarly: Writing assistance
- Zotero: Citation management

Mathematics:
- Wolfram Alpha: Calculations and explanations
- Desmos: Graphing calculator
- Paul's Online Math Notes: Calculus and algebra
- Symbolab: Step-by-step math solutions
