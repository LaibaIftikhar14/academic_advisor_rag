Plagiarism Detection and Prevention Policy

The university uses advanced plagiarism detection software for all submitted assignments and papers. This software compares submissions against:
- Previously submitted student work
- Published academic papers and journals
- Online sources and websites
- AI-generated content databases

Acceptable use of sources:
- All sources must be properly cited using the required citation style
- Direct quotes must be in quotation marks with page numbers
- Paraphrasing requires citation even if words are changed
- Ideas taken from others must be attributed

AI-generated content:
- Using AI tools to generate assignment content without disclosure is considered plagiarism
- Students must disclose any AI assistance used in their work
- The extent of AI use must be appropriate to the assignment guidelines

First offense: Zero on the assignment
Second offense: Fail the course
Third offense: Academic suspension
