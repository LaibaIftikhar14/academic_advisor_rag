Preparing for Internships and Career Development

University is the best time to prepare for your professional career.

Building your resume:
- Include relevant coursework and projects
- List technical skills clearly
- Include any volunteer or part-time work experience
- Keep it to one page for early career
- Tailor your resume for each application

Portfolio development:
- Create a GitHub profile with your best projects
- Write documentation for each project
- Include variety: web apps, algorithms, data analysis
- Contribute to open source projects
- Build a personal website

Internship search strategies:
- Start applying 6 months before the internship period
- Use LinkedIn, Indeed, and company websites
- Attend university career fairs
- Network with alumni through university programs
- Ask professors for industry connections

Interview preparation:
- Practice common technical interview questions
- Review data structures and algorithms
- Practice system design questions for senior roles
- Prepare behavioral answers using STAR method
- Research the company thoroughly before interviews

Professional networking:
- LinkedIn profile with professional photo
- Connect with classmates, professors, and professionals
- Attend industry events and meetups
- Join relevant professional organizations
- Follow companies and thought leaders in your field
