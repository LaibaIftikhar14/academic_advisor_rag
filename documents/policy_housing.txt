Student Housing and Residence Life Policy

On-campus housing options:
- Freshman dormitories (required for first-year students)
- Upperclassman residence halls
- Graduate student apartments
- Family housing for students with dependents

Housing application:
- Applications open March 1st for following academic year
- Priority given to freshmen and students with documented need
- Lottery system for upperclassman housing
- Off-campus housing list available through student affairs

Residence hall rules:
- Quiet hours: 10 PM - 8 AM on weekdays, 12 AM - 10 AM on weekends
- Guests must be registered at the front desk
- No cooking in rooms (kitchens available on each floor)
- No pets except fish in tanks under 10 gallons
- Alcohol prohibited for students under 21

Roommate conflicts:
- Contact your RA (Resident Advisor) first
- Mediation services available through residence life office
- Room changes possible after the first 4 weeks of semester
- Document all conflicts in writing

Maintenance requests:
- Submit online through housing portal
- Emergency maintenance: Call security desk
- Response time: 24 hours for standard requests, immediate for emergencies
