International Student Policies and Support

Visa requirements:
- F-1 visa required for full-time international students
- Must maintain full-time enrollment (minimum 12 credits)
- Work authorization (CPT/OPT) requires application through international office
- Notify international office of any change in address or status within 10 days

Required documents:
- Valid passport (at least 6 months beyond program end date)
- Current I-20 or DS-2019 form
- Proof of financial support
- Health insurance enrollment confirmation

English language support:
- English as a Second Language (ESL) courses available
- Free English tutoring through international student office
- Language exchange program with domestic students
- Academic writing support specifically for non-native speakers

Cultural adjustment support:
- International student orientation at start of each semester
- International Student Association (ISA) social events
- Peer mentor program pairing new and experienced international students
- Cultural celebration events throughout the year

Financial matters:
- International students not eligible for federal financial aid
- Institutional scholarships available (merit-based)
- On-campus employment limited to 20 hours per week during semester
- Off-campus employment requires specific work authorization
