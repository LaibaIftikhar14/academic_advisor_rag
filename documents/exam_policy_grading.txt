Grading Policy and Grade Scale

University Standard Grade Scale:
A+ : 95-100% (4.0 GPA)
A  : 90-94%  (4.0 GPA)
A- : 85-89%  (3.7 GPA)
B+ : 80-84%  (3.3 GPA)
B  : 75-79%  (3.0 GPA)
B- : 70-74%  (2.7 GPA)
C+ : 65-69%  (2.3 GPA)
C  : 60-64%  (2.0 GPA)
F  : Below 60% (0.0 GPA)

Grade Appeals:
Students who believe their grade is incorrect may submit a grade appeal within 2 weeks of receiving their final grade. Appeals must be submitted in writing to the department chair with specific reasons for the appeal.

Incomplete Grades:
An incomplete grade (I) may be granted if a student cannot complete coursework due to documented circumstances beyond their control.
