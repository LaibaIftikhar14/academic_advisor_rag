Student Health Services Policy

University Health Center services:
- Primary care and sick visits
- Mental health counseling
- Immunization clinics
- Sexual health services
- Nutrition counseling
- Physical therapy referrals

Hours and appointments:
- Walk-in hours: Monday-Friday 8 AM - 10 AM
- Appointments: Monday-Friday 10 AM - 5 PM
- After hours: Campus emergency services or local urgent care
- Telehealth appointments available

Required immunizations:
All students must provide documentation of:
- MMR (measles, mumps, rubella)
- Varicella (chickenpox)
- Meningococcal vaccine
- COVID-19 vaccination (current policy)
- TB screening (international students)

Mental health services:
- Individual counseling: Up to 12 sessions per year
- Group therapy: Various support groups available
- Crisis intervention: 24/7 crisis line available
- Psychiatric services: Medication management available
- Referrals to community mental health when needed

Insurance:
- University health insurance plan available
- International students required to enroll
- Domestic students may waive with comparable coverage
- Dental and vision plans available separately
