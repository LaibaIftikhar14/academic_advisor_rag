Final Examination Policy

Final examinations are held during the designated final exam period at the end of each semester. The final exam schedule is published at least 4 weeks before the exam period begins.

Important rules:
- Final exams cannot be rescheduled except in extreme circumstances
- Students with three or more finals on the same day may request alternative scheduling
- Final exam locations may differ from regular classroom locations
- Students must check the official exam schedule regularly for any changes

Preparation recommendations:
- Begin reviewing material at least 2 weeks before finals
- Attend all review sessions offered by instructors
- Form study groups with classmates
- Visit office hours for clarification on difficult topics
- Get adequate sleep before each exam
