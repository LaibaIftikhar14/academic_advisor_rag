Makeup Exam Policy

Students who miss a scheduled examination due to documented illness, family emergency, or other valid reason may apply for a makeup exam.

To apply for a makeup exam:
1. Contact your course instructor within 48 hours of the missed exam
2. Submit a formal makeup exam request form to the registrar
3. Provide supporting documentation (medical certificate, death certificate, etc.)
4. Wait for approval from the department chair

Makeup exams are typically scheduled within two weeks of the original exam date. The makeup exam may be in a different format than the original. Only one makeup exam is permitted per course per semester.

Requests submitted after 48 hours will not be considered without exceptional circumstances.
