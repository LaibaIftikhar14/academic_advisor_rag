General Exam Policy - University Academic Regulations

All students must present their valid student ID card at every examination. Failure to produce ID may result in disqualification from the exam.

Examinations begin exactly at the scheduled time. Students arriving more than 15 minutes late will not be permitted to enter the examination hall.

Mobile phones and electronic devices must be switched off and placed in bags before entering the examination hall. Any student found using a mobile phone during an exam will receive a zero grade.

Students must follow the seating plan provided by the invigilator. Changing seats without permission is considered misconduct.

Writing must stop immediately when the invigilator announces the end of the exam. Continuing to write after time is called is considered cheating.
