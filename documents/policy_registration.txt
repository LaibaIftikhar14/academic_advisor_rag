Course Registration Policy

Registration periods:
- Continuing students: Registration opens 4 weeks before semester start
- New students: Registration during orientation week
- Late registration: Available for 2 weeks after semester start with late fee

Registration rules:
- Students must maintain minimum 12 credits per semester for full-time status
- Maximum 18 credits per semester without special permission
- Prerequisites must be completed before registering for advanced courses
- Some courses require instructor permission to register

Adding and dropping courses:
- Add period: First 2 weeks of semester (no academic record)
- Drop period: Weeks 3-8 (W grade appears on transcript)
- Medical withdrawal: After week 8 with documentation (WM grade)
- No withdrawals accepted after week 12 without exceptional approval

Registration holds:
Common reasons for registration holds:
- Outstanding tuition balance
- Library fines exceeding $50
- Unpaid parking tickets
- Missing immunization records
- Academic probation

Contact the registrar's office immediately if you have a registration hold.
