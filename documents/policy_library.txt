University Library Policies and Resources

Library hours:
- Monday to Thursday: 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM
- Friday: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Sunday: 12:00 PM - 10:00 PM
- Extended hours during exam periods

Borrowing privileges:
- Undergraduate students: 10 books for 3 weeks
- Graduate students: 20 books for 6 weeks
- Renewals: Up to 3 times if no holds placed
- Fines: $0.25 per day per item

Library services:
- Interlibrary loan: Request books from other libraries
- Research consultation: Meet with subject librarian
- Citation help: Assistance with APA, MLA, Chicago
- Printing and scanning: Available at help desk
- Study rooms: Book online up to 1 week in advance
- 24-hour computer lab: Access with student ID

Online resources:
- Access to over 100 academic databases
- E-book collection of 500,000+ titles
- Streaming video platforms for educational content
- Remote access available with student login

Academic support through library:
- Writing tutors available at the library
- Statistics tutoring by appointment
- Research workshops every semester
- Thesis and dissertation support for graduate students
